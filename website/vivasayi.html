Crop Recommendation

Crop recommendation for agricultural land in Tamil Nadu involves analyzing various factors such as soil type, climate conditions, water availability, and market demand to determine the most suitable crops for cultivation.

The first step in crop recommendation is soil testing. Different regions in Tamil Nadu have varying soil types, including red soil, black soil, alluvial soil, and laterite soil. Soil testing helps in determining the pH level, nutrient content, and soil texture, which are crucial factors for crop selection.
